About Juice WRLD

Juice WRLD, born Jarad Higgins, was an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. He received several rewards such as 2019 billboard best new artist and was nominated 2020 billboard top 100.

Some of His Albums:

  • Goodbye & Good Riddance (2018)
  • Death Race for Love (2019)
  • Legends Never Die (2020)
  • Fighting Demons (2021)
  • The Party Never Ends (2024)
